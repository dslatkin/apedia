April 23, 2003
As some of you may know, I am now helping with the development side of Akarra. It is fortunate for me because I have the chance to show off my talent and I'm confident with my abilities. The downside is that I can no longer update this site the way I used to as a "normal" player, but that doesn't bother me because I've decided that the Akarrian Encyclopedia will be a semi-player-driven fansite. I will perform the updates from the content that you submit to me (via email or IRC, etc).

Please note that I will not post any updates regarding maps, quests, or certain items which may give clues to certain spoiler information for obvious reasons. Some time in the future I will post a small section describing in detail how the Akarrian Encyclopedia will function.

I also have to thank Kilala, GameJunker, Talonz, and everyone in the community for recently submitting a lot of content. It's a start.....and a good one! :)

April 15, 2003

As usual, I've added lots more stuff so have fun and check it out :)

Many of you have asked me if I will be making a craft guide and the answer is no. I'm sorry but I have my reasons....which is why I completely removed all of the quests. The Akarrian Encyclopedia was not meant to be a walkthrough site. It is supposed to resemble a "game manual" with a huge database of various things and ditch the walkthrough parts but still give just enough information to keep the pace of excitement. It's more fun that way :)
