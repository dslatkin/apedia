June 7, 2003

New section completed.

The new screenshots section contains three main parts. :) The normal screenshots, the N'Geo Event screenshots, and the Nelly Hoards screenshots.

The normal screenshots can be submitted by anybody, as long as they are interesting and unique, I'll put them up.

The N'Geo was a surprise event that happened in Hope over a weekend. A taratel was summoned and the citizens of Akarra were challenged to defeat it. It put up a good fight, but Hope won in the end.

The Nelly Hoards was just a day that Nelly summoned a bunch of monsters. These included zombies, walking dead, skeletons, nightfighters, Djinnis, imps, and even rabbits. A lot of coins that day were lost. :)

Enjoy the new section!
