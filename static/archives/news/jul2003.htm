July 26, 2003

A hidden summoning near the Rimsin Castle was carried out a few days ago. It revealed two very high leveled monster, the Master Tarian (level 120) and the Tarian Sentinel (level 85). Very hard monsters. These have been added to the monster page. The Tarian Sentinel emits darkness, I added those notes to the Nightfighers, Djinni, and Sentinels.

Added a new weapon called the Flamebringer Glaive. It looks quite cool, but it's not worth the 40 faith requirement. On average, it'll do about an extra 10 damage on Iwids from its Item Effect. You'll be killing Iwids by the time you get the requirements.

Added the screenshots to the screenshot section now! Over 30 screenshots for your viewing pleasure.

Also changed the Misc section on the items page to Mounts. Updated the picture of sliver of bronze. Updated menu bar because of the changes too.
